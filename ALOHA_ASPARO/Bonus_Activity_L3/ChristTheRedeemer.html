7 Wonders of the World

Christ The Redeemer - Brazil

Christ the Redeemer is a colossal Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. It stands atop Corcovado Mountain, overlooking the city, and is one of the New Seven Wonders of the World. The statue was designed by Heitor da Silva Costa and sculpted by Paul Landowski, with Gheorghe Leonida sculpting the face.
